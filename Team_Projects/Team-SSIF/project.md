## Overview

Team SSSIF wishes to compared labor-market conditions across eight countries at different income levels, examining how salaries, employment counts, and skill levels vary between public and private sectors.

## Challenge Category Area

Category IV: The Public-Private Jobs Nexus

Team SSSIF selected a diverse group of eight countries representing different income levelsâ€”lower-income (Mozambique, Nepal), lower-middle income (Bangladesh, India), upper-middle income (Brazil, China), and high-income (Germany, USA).
